An instrument, not a calculator.

Every unit you type is tokenised, weighted by SI exponents, and reduced to a base dimension vector. Convert across unit families, validate derived quantities, and trust the math.

  • Symbolic expression parser (kg·m·s⁻², km/h², J·K⁻¹·mol⁻¹, …)
  • SI base dimension vector for every quantity
  • Local-only — no servers, no accounts, no analytics
  • Engineered for engineers, scientists and students
