Privacy Policy

Last updated · January 9, 2025 · Effective immediately

TOGIPPE (“the app”, “we”, “our”) is designed to operate entirely on your device. This notice explains what data is processed, where it lives, and how you can control it. It applies to the iOS edition of TOGIPPE distributed via the Apple App Store.

1. Overview

We do not maintain accounts, do not require sign-in, and do not transmit your conversion inputs, hunt history, or preferences to any external server. Your data stays on your iPhone or iPad unless you explicitly share it.

Unlike a basic calculator or unit converter, TOGIPPE performs symbolic dimensional analysis: it parses any user-typed unit expression (for example, kg·m·s⁻²) and returns the SI base dimension vector (M¹·L¹·T⁻² for force). All parsing and math is computed locally on-device.

Short version: no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no network content requests.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Personal identifiers

We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or location.

2.2 Analytics & tracking

The app does not embed third-party analytics SDKs and does not record behavioural events. No advertising identifiers are read or written.

2.3 Network requests

Conversions and unit math are computed locally; no user input is sent over the network. The only outbound traffic is standard Apple App Store receipt validation and crash-log submission performed by the iOS platform itself, outside our control.

2.4 Local storage (on your device)

The following data is stored in the standard iOS app sandbox using UserDefaults:

  • Recent hunts — up to 32 recently typed unit expressions, so you can revisit them between launches. Stored under the key hunter.history.v1. User Content.
  • Display precision — index for the precision selector. Preferences.
  • SI notation toggle — whether results prefer SI base symbols. Preferences.
  • Persist-history toggle — whether recent hunts are kept between launches. Preferences.
  • Haptics toggle — whether haptic feedback is enabled. Preferences.

None of these items are linked to a user identity, are not used for tracking, and never leave the device.

3. Permissions

The app requests no system permissions. It does not access the camera, microphone, photo library, contacts, calendars, location services, motion sensors, Bluetooth, local network, or any background mode.

4. Your Rights & Controls

4.1 Access & export

All data is local; you can view your recent hunts inside the app at any time.

4.2 Deletion

Use Settings → Data → Clear Recent Hunts to erase the local hunt history. Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from the device sandbox.

4.3 Opt-out of persistence

Disable Persist Recent Hunts in Settings to ensure nothing is stored between launches.

4.4 Data portability

Because all data lives in UserDefaults inside the app sandbox, exporting requires either an in-app share-sheet (not currently shipped) or a device backup through standard iOS tools.

5. Accuracy Disclaimer

TOGIPPE is provided as a productivity and reference tool. While we strive to keep physical constants, unit definitions and conversion factors accurate and up to date with recognised standards (SI, NIST), the app is intended for engineering, scientific and educational assistance — not for safety-critical, medical, legal, financial or life-dependent calculations.

Always verify critical results against authoritative sources. The developers assume no liability for damages arising from the use of, or the inability to use, the converted values.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and knowingly collects no information from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us so we can assist.

We may update this notice to reflect changes in the app or applicable law. Updates will be published on this page and, where required, surfaced through App Store update notes. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
